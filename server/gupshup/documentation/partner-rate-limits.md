# Partner Rate Limits

Effective from 28th Sep 2023, Gupshup introduced rate limits to partner APIs for platform reliability.

## Default Limits
- **General Rate**: 10 requests per 1 second for most APIs.
- **Cooldown**: 1 second (per second limits) or 1 minute (per minute limits).

Specific endpoints may have different limits. Partners with high-volume requirements should contact their local CSM.

[Source: Gupshup Documentation](https://partner-docs.gupshup.io/docs/partner-rate-limits)

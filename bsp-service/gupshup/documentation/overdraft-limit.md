# Overdraft Limit

The Overdraft Limit allows wallet owners to manage their balance more effectively by allowing it to go negative within a predefined limit.

## Setting Up Overdraft Limit
- **Navigation**: Partner Portal > My Wallet > Modify Overdraft Limit.
- **Range**: Minimum 0 to Maximum 1000 credits.
- **Assignment**: Credit is added to the Total Overdraft Limit after confirmation.

## Overdraft Limit Calculation
- Based on total recharges and usage over the last 90 days.
- **Gupshup Assigned Overdraft**: Special cases handled by Gupshup support/CSMs based on existing relationships. These cannot be modified by the user.

[Source: Gupshup Documentation](https://partner-docs.gupshup.io/docs/overdraft-limit)

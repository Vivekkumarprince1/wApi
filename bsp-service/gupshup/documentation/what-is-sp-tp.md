# Solution Partners & Tech Providers

## Solution Partners (SP)
Solution Partners provide WhatsApp Business Platform services to other businesses. They manage the credit line extended to businesses, removing the need for businesses to enter their own payment methods. They also have access to dedicated support from Meta for quick resolution of concerns.

## Tech Providers (TP)
The Tech Provider Program enables third-party developers (ISVs) to work with a Solution Provider like Gupshup. It allows businesses to leverage Meta's ecosystem and develop joint solutions.

### Benefits of Tech Provider Program
- **Account Management**: Permission to manage an end business's account and perform actions (manage templates, receive webhook events, etc.) on behalf of the business.
- **Embedded Signup**: Tech Providers can onboard businesses on their own platform using Embedded Signup or delegate it to Gupshup.
- **Tech Partner Status**: Eligibility to become a Tech Partner with access to the Meta Partner Directory and marketing/training resources.
- **Credit Line Access**: Continued access to the Solution Partner's (Gupshup's) credit line.

[Source: Gupshup Documentation](https://partner-docs.gupshup.io/docs/what-is-sp-tp)

# Wallet & Commissions

## Wallet (Prepaid Billing)
The Gupshup wallet works in USD and is used for prepaid deductions based on app usage.

### Wallet Usage
- **Add Credits**: 1 credit = 1 USD. Min $10, Max $10,000 per transaction.
- **Payment Methods**: Stripe or Ebanx.
- **Automatic Deductions**: WhatsApp and Gupshup fees are automatically deducted.
- **Monitoring**: Alerts are triggered to the billing email when the balance reaches $5.

## Commissions
Partners earn commissions based on their model.
- **Unused Commission Policy**: Commissions earned between April (Year 1) and March (Year 2) must be redeemed by March (Year 3). Unredeemed commissions expire.

[Source: Gupshup Documentation](https://partner-docs.gupshup.io/docs/commissions)

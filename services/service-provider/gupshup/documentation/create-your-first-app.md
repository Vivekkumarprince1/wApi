# Create Your First App

## Steps in Partner Portal
1. **Apps Dashboard**: Login and navigate to the Apps Dashboard.
2. **Create New App**: Name the app.
3. **Go Live**: Find the app in the dashboard and select "Go Live".
4. **Onboarding**: Fill in necessary data.
5. **Embedded Flow**: Complete the Meta Embedded Signup flow in the 3rd step.
6. **Live**: The app will be live for messaging.

[Source: Gupshup Documentation](https://partner-docs.gupshup.io/docs/create-your-first-app)

# Introduction to Partner Portal

The Gupshup Partner Portal enables ISVs and Tech Providers to manage their partnership with Gupshup and WhatsApp.

## Key Capabilities
- **WABA Management**: Create and manage WhatsApp Business Accounts for customers.
- **Analytics**: Monitor message statuses (Sent, Delivered, Read, Failed) over time.
- **Billing**: Manage the prepaid Wallet for usage deductions.
- **Custom UI**: Partners can use the exposing APIs to build their own branded interfaces or use the white-labeled **Partner Customer Portal**.

[Source: Gupshup Documentation](https://partner-docs.gupshup.io/docs/introduction)
